Tweetbeat

A fun Node.js project written in 24 hours during the MLH Prime: Southwest Regional. Watches a set Twitter hashtag for tweets and analyzes the mood from the tweet to generate a melody. Melodies are generated with using Markov chains and chord progressions. Utilizes express, socket.io, twit, Tone.js, and the Watson Tone Analyzer API. Written by Xanthe Hammer, Kathryn Gray, and Steven Peralta.
